## Guiding Questions for Each Assumption
- Is this truly impossible, or just "not done"?
- What physical or logical law forces this?
- Who benefits from this belief staying alive?
- If we had unlimited resources, would this assumption still hold?
- What would a Martian who has never seen this product assume?

## Example (for a conventional car)
| # | Assumption | Source | Verdict | If Destroyed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Must have a steering wheel | Tradition | Destroy | Explore joystick, neural control, or autonomous path selection |
| 2 | Must run on fossil fuel | Tradition | Destroy | Open to electric, hydrogen, compressed air, kinetic flywheel |
| 3 | Must have 4 wheels | Physical Law (stability) | Keep (for ground contact) | But explore 3-wheel or leg-based designs if stability can be solved |
